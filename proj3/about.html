Located in Los Angeles, California, this popular restaurant has become one of the top dining spots in the U.S. Known for its innovative Asian fusion cuisine, EZO was founded by Jan Patel and Lana Tran in the early 2000s and continues to thrive in 2025.


Blending Vietnamese, Japanese, and Filipino flavors, the restaurant offers a vibrant, festive space that welcomes everyone to enjoy traditional dishes in a modern and inviting atmosphere.
