Located in Los Angeles, California, this popular restaurant has become a top dining spot in the U.S. Known for its growing Asian fusion cuisine, it was founded by Jan Patel and Lana Tran in the early 2000s and continues to thrive in 2025. Blending Vietnamese, Japanese, and Filipino flavors, the restaurant offers a vibrant, festive space that welcomes everyone to enjoy traditional dishes in a fun and inviting atmosphere.
