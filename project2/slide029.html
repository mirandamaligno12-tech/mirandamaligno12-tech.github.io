Make creamy, rich gelato at home without any fancy machines!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract

Instructions (By Hand!)

  • Heat milk and cream gently in a saucepan until steaming, not boiling.
  • In a bowl, whisk egg yolks with sugar until pale and smooth.
  • Slowly add a bit of hot milk mixture to yolks while whisking (tempering eggs).
  • Gradually whisk tempered yolks back into saucepan with remaining milk mixture.
  • Cook on low heat, stirring constantly, until thick enough to coat a spoon (~170°F or 77°C).
  • Remove from heat, stir in vanilla extract.
  • Strain mixture into a bowl, cool to room temperature.
  • Cover and chill in the fridge for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  • Pour chilled custard into a shallow container.
  • Freeze for 30 minutes, then vigorously stir or whisk to break ice crystals.
  • Repeat freezing and stirring every 30 minutes for 2-3 hours until creamy.
  • Serve immediately or keep covered in freezer.

Tips from Nonna

  • Use fresh eggs to ensure safety and flavor.
  • Don’t skip stirring during freezing — it makes gelato smooth.
  • Add cocoa powder, coffee, or fruit puree to customize flavor.
  • Let gelato soften slightly before serving for best texture.
