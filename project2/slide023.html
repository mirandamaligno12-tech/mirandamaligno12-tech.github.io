A classic Italian dessert layered with coffee, mascarpone, and love.

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • 1 cup (240g) mascarpone cheese, room temperature
  • 2 cups (480ml) strong brewed coffee or espresso, cooled
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 1 pack (200g) ladyfinger cookies (Savoiardi)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Dark chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions

  • In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar together until thick and pale.
  • In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  • Gently fold mascarpone into the egg mixture.
  • Fold whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until smooth.
  • In a shallow dish, mix coffee and liqueur (if using).
  • Dip each ladyfinger briefly into the coffee mixture (don't soak).
  • Arrange soaked ladyfingers in a layer in your serving dish.
  • Spread half of the mascarpone cream mixture over the ladyfingers.
  • Repeat with another layer of soaked ladyfingers and the rest of the cream.
  • Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (or overnight).
  • Dust with cocoa powder and top with chocolate shavings before serving.

Tips

  • Use high-quality mascarpone for best flavor and texture.
  • Don't soak ladyfingers too long or the dessert will get soggy.
