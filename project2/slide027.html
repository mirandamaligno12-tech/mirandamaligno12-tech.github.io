A creamy, dreamy frozen Italian dessert — like ice cream, but fancier!

Ingredients

Base:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 cups (480ml) heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Optional Add-ins:

  • Crushed amaretti or biscotti
  • Chopped dark chocolate or chocolate chips
  • Fresh berries
  • Toasted nuts (pistachios or almonds)
  • Espresso powder or a splash of liqueur (like amaretto or limoncello)

Instructions

  1. Separate the eggs — yolks in one bowl, whites in another.
  2. Whisk the yolks with half of the sugar until pale and thick.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the egg whites with a pinch of salt until soft peaks form.
  4. Gradually add the remaining sugar to the whites and beat to stiff peaks.
  5. In another bowl, whip the cream until it holds soft peaks. Add vanilla extract.
  6. Gently fold the whipped cream into the yolk mixture.
  7. Fold in the meringue (egg whites + sugar) carefully to keep the mixture airy.
  8. Add any extras you like: chocolate, nuts, crushed cookies, berries, etc.
  9. Pour the mixture into a loaf pan lined with plastic wrap or parchment.
  10. Cover and freeze for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  11. Slice and serve straight from the freezer — no churning needed!

Tips from Nonna

  • Use very fresh eggs (or pasteurized, since they’re not cooked)
  • Don’t skip the folding steps keep it light and airy!
  • Want it fancier? Drizzle melted chocolate on top before freezing.
  • Serve with espresso, fresh fruit, or a biscotti on the side
