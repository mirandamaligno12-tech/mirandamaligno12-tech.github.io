Lemon Granita (Granita al Limone)

A refreshing, icy Italian dessert — simple, tangy, and perfect after a meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (500 ml) water
  • 1 cup (200 g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240 ml) fresh lemon juice (from 4–6 lemons)
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Make syrup: In a saucepan, combine water and sugar. Heat over medium, stirring until sugar is dissolved. Let cool.
  2. Add lemon: Stir in lemon juice, zest (if using), and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  3. Freeze: Pour into a shallow baking dish. Place in the freezer for 30–45 minutes.
  4. Scrape: When ice starts to form at the edges, scrape with a fork. Return to freezer and repeat scraping every 30 minutes for 3–4 hours, until light and flaky.
  5. Serve: Scoop into chilled glasses or bowls. Serve immediately, or freeze and scrape again before serving.

Optional Twists:

  • Add mint leaves for freshness
  • Splash in limoncello for a boozy version
  • Try orange or grapefruit juice for variation
