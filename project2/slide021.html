Affogato

Italy’s easiest and most elegant dessert: Coffee meets ice cream!

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of high-quality vanilla gelato or ice cream
  • 1 shot (30–45 ml) of hot, freshly brewed espresso
  • Optional: shaved chocolate, crushed biscotti, or a splash of liqueur (Amaretto)

Instructions

  • Chill a small glass or bowl in the freezer for 5–10 minutes.
  • Add one generous scoop of vanilla gelato to the glass.
  • Brew a fresh shot of espresso.
  • Pour the hot espresso directly over the gelato just before serving.
  • Optionally, add chocolate shavings or drizzle liqueur on top.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the hot-and-cold magic!

Tips

  • Use freshly brewed espresso – no instant coffee!
  • Try hazelnut or coffee-flavored gelato for variety.
  • Serve in a clear glass for a fancy presentation.
